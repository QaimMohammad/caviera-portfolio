§ 04

Questions you're already asking.

Straight answers
No fine print
Q.01 How many revisions do I get? +

Base packages include up to 3 revision rounds. Higher and custom packages include unlimited revisions within the agreed scope.

Scope creep is handled separately — if you ask for something that wasn't in the original brief, I'll tell you before any extra work gets done.

Q.02 What's your refund policy? +

Full refund within 24 hours of paying the advance, no questions asked.

After that, if you're unhappy with the first draft, I'll revise until it's right — and if we genuinely can't get there, I'll refund. I'd rather return money than ship something neither of us is proud of.

Q.03 How do payments work? +

Default is 50% upfront, 50% on delivery. For situations where I have leverage (returning clients, smaller scopes), I sometimes work 100% on delivery — we'll figure out what makes sense.

I accept Payoneer, PayPal, and direct bank transfer.

Q.04 Do you sign NDAs? +

Yes. Most of my projects are under NDA — happy to sign yours before we discuss any details.

Q.05 Who owns the code? +

You do. After final payment, full ownership of the code transfers to you.

I only ask permission to reference the work in my portfolio. If you'd rather keep it private, it stays private.

Q.06 What if I hate the first draft? +

We revise, or you get your money back — your call.

I'd rather refund than push through a project that isn't working for you.

Q.07 What if I cancel mid-project? +

Depends on where we are. I keep up to 25% of the deposit to cover time already spent; the rest is refunded.

Exact split scales with project complexity and what's already shipped — we'll sort it fairly.
